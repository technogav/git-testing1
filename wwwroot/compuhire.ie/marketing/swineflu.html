Act Now!

Right now many organizations and businesses are reviewing their Business Continuity & Disaster Recovery Plans due to the increased threats posed by Swine-Flu H1N1 Virus.

Here are some basic tips to help you plan ahead for both Small & Large businesses.

Think Disaster and begin to look at how your business would be affected if your key people and/or facilities were unable to continue working.

■   What are the Minimum Level of Services & Operations acceptable to keep your business functioning and customers maintained?
■Identify Your Key People. Who are essential to keeping the minimum level of services & operations going?
■Plan Ahead. Can you isolate this group away from the main business team?
■Can some people work from home or at another facility?
■Draw up a checklist of what you need to have in place to cover such a scenario.

For Example.

■   Can you arrange a nearby Hotel for the Key Team to work from?
■Can you arrange for Hardware & Equipment to be at the new location quickly?
■Do you have sufficient access to Data & Business Software?
■Who will handle Telephone calls?
■Can Telephone Calls be re-routed to a New Location?
■What about Deliveries & Inbound Materials, Stock etc for the Business?
